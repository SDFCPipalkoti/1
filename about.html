SEMAL DALA FOOTBALL CLUB

SDFC, formed in 2020, is a passionate football club dedicated to fostering talent and promoting the sport within the community. Over the years, SDFC has become a vital part of local football, organizing events and participating in tournaments that showcase its commitment to the game. The club continues to inspire young athletes and contribute significantly to the growth of football in the region.SDFC's emphasis on teamwork and sportsmanship has earned it a loyal following and respect within the football community. As the club continues to grow, it remains focused on developing future stars and enhancing the sport's presence in the area.
